About this website

Our research team developed some deep learning methods for protein science. We originally hoped to build a website to provide corresponding online services for everyone. But for some reasons, we have suspended these online services. If you want to use these methods, you can download the corresponding programs from GitHub.

CSI-LSTM

Its citation is J Biomol NMR 2021, 75 (10-12), 393-400.
